Amy's Golf Website

I’ll admit it: I’ve never actually been good at golf. Like, at all. My golf ball has a mysterious talent for finding sand traps, water hazards, and occasionally even trees. But despite my lack of Tiger Woods-level skills, there's something about golf that just makes sense to me. Plus, who doesn’t love the aesthetic of crisp greens, pastel polos, and those tiny little flags waving in the breeze?

So, when I was brainstorming a theme for my website, it hit me: golf. It’s a quirky, charming metaphor for navigating life—sometimes you hit a hole-in-one, sometimes you end up in the rough, but you always keep swinging. Perfect, right?
