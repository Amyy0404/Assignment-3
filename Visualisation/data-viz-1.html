TOP GOLF PLAYERS OF 2024

In this data visualization, I utilized D3.js to present the top golf player rankings for 2024, focusing on their average points, total points, and event participation. The visualization is designed as an interactive bar chart, pulling data from RapidAPI, which provides live updates on golf stats for the current year. By using D3, the chart displays each player's ranking and performance metrics, with the flexibility to show varying numbers of players based on user selection.

The bar chart effectively communicates each player's average points, visually emphasizing the leading players by adjusting bar lengths proportional to their performance. This method is especially effective because it allows users to quickly grasp relative player rankings while offering additional depth through interactive tooltips. When hovering over a bar, detailed information about each player, such as their rank, average points, total points gained, and number of events played, appears in a styled tooltip.

The inclusion of user interaction—changing the number of displayed players and dynamically updating the chart—enhances the effectiveness of this visualization. It allows users to explore the data at their preferred level of detail. The choice of bar colors, hover effects, and tooltip styles also ensures that the chart remains visually appealing and consistent with the overall golf theme of the website.
